About Sweet Scents Perfumery

At Sweet Scents Perfumery, we believe that fragrance is more than just a scent—it's an experience. Founded in 2010 by a team of passionate perfumers and scent enthusiasts, our mission is to create perfumes that transport you to a world of sweetness and wonder. Inspired by childhood memories of candy shops, beachside strolls, and ethereal dreams, our fragrances are crafted with the finest ingredients to ensure longevity, allure, and a touch of magic. Whether you're looking for a playful, sugary note or a sophisticated, marshmallow-like indulgence, we have something for everyone. Our perfumes are vegan, cruelty-free, and made with sustainable practices in mind. Explore our collection below and let your senses guide you to your next favorite scent.

We offer a wide range of products, including eau de parfum, eau de toilette, and body mists. Each fragrance is bottled in elegant, reusable glass vials that add a touch of luxury to your daily routine. Our scents are perfect for special occasions, everyday wear, or as thoughtful gifts for loved ones. Sweet Candy

Transport yourself to a candy store wonderland with Sweet Candy, our flagship fragrance. Perfect for those who crave a playful, nostalgic aroma, Sweet Candy is ideal for spring and summer outings. It's a versatile eau de parfum that transitions seamlessly from daytime fun to evening elegance. Many of our customers describe it as "addictively sweet yet not overpowering," making it a staple in any fragrance wardrobe. Crafted with natural essences and a hint of edible-inspired ingredients, this perfume is cruelty-free and long-lasting, with up to 8 hours of scent throw. Pair it with light, airy outfits for a youthful vibe, or layer it with our body lotion for intensified allure. Available in 50ml and 100ml bottles.

About Us

Sweet Scents Perfumery was born from a love for the simple joys of life—candy, beaches, and dreams. Our founder, a former pastry chef turned perfumer, drew inspiration from global travels and childhood memories to create scents that are both innovative and nostalgic. We operate from a sustainable studio in California, where we hand-blend each fragrance using only the finest, ethically sourced ingredients. Our team includes expert noses, chemists, and artists who collaborate to bring you scents that tell a story. We're committed to transparency, with all ingredients listed on our website, and we donate a portion of profits to environmental causes. Join us in celebrating the art of scent-making.
